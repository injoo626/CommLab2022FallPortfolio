"30 Minutes Film Festival"

We have all been newcomers at some point in our lives.
First days are always a odd mixture of anxiety and excitement.

How do you remember those moments?
How were you welcomed? How did you welcome others?
Marhaba captures the excitement and anxiety of the first week of univertiy.

The website features the film and the 30 Minute Film Festival which invites videographers around the world to share their work produced in thirty minutes.

"The Normalcy Test"

What is considered "normal" in NYUAD?

Is it normal to eat alone in D2? Is it normal to stay in your room instead of partying? How should a "normal" student behave on this campus?

"Normalcy Test" twists this belief that one common norm exists. The project initially invites users to test their normalcy by answering how they would behave in the given situations. In the end, however, the website reminds that every individual is unique and there is no need to struggle to fit in.

"InternalRecording_AlexaN978384"

Have you ever lost someone close?

This project captures the process of the character going through a loss of her brother Bilal.

It does not tell the audience any context in the beginning. As a user listens to the internal recordings of Alexa on different dates, however, a user slowly understands what the chracter is going through

"The Whereabouts of
the Missing *****"

This project sheds light on the laundry theft incidents on campus through a fictional story revolving around three characters: victim, suspect, and a clueless cat lover who accidently became a part of the wrong interview.

The audience never gets to know what actually happened in the laundry room; they only have to guess the story based on the two completely different point of views. Did Chad really steal Mariette's underwear? Or is Mariette accusing an innocent person?
